Images

This page covers common image formats, but you may also be looking for links and useful extras.

JPGs

JPG images are good for photos, but lack transparency.

GIFs

GIF images are good for animations, but have limited color palettes.

PNGs

PNG images are good for anything that isn't a photo or animated.

SVGs

SVG images are vector-based, so can scale up or down to any size without loss of quality.
